Turkey Goulash

Description

The dirtiest, greasiest noods you'll find. We're talking greeeeeeeasy.

Ingredients

    1 packet noods
    30g spicy chorizo
    1 large egg
    3 spring onions
    A few small mushrooms

Steps

    Cook noodles as described on the packet
    Dice the chorizo
    Dry fry the diced chorizo for a few minutes
    Wash and slice the vegetable
    Once the chorizo is mostly fried, slide to the edge of the pan and fry the egg, then the sliced mushrooms, in the chorizo oil/fat
    Add everything apart from the egg to the serving bowl and mix around
    Add the egg on top and serve
